\textbf{Computer Engineering Program:}
The Computer Engineering program at ESPRIT provides comprehensive education in both hardware and software aspects of computing systems. The night school format allows working professionals and students to pursue their engineering degree while maintaining other commitments. The curriculum covers:
\begin{itemize}
\item Software Engineering and Development
\item Computer Systems and Architecture
\item Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
\item Cybersecurity and Network Systems
\item Mobile and Web Technologies
\item Project Management and Innovation
\end{itemize}

The 4-year night school program is designed to accommodate students who prefer evening classes, offering the same rigorous curriculum and academic standards as the traditional daytime program, with flexible scheduling that supports work-study balance.
